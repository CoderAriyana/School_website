    Founder's Message

    Harpreet Singh Cheema


    Hello All..!
    Its been a very rewarding and interesting journey and we look forward to some great experiences and success, in future, with your cooperation, support and blessings. We started with only 14 children on 13th April, 2008, but, we are sure that you will all agree that today we are a much sought for school in the tricity. Our Endeavour is to provide best of education through well researched teaching methodologies and an excellent teacher- student rapport and ratio. We are affiliated with IB- International Baccalaureate for PYP program and with Cambridge Assessment International Education for IGCSE Assessments. Our motto “We don’t teach- We educate” will continue to remain very close to our heart. Our focus shall remain on the intellectual, physical and emotional well being of our students. With the Mission Statement “India for World” our commitment on world class education becomes even stronger. We take pride in our staff and faculty for their unconditional support at all time and their untiring efforts to give the best of education to the children. Thanks to the entire FirstSteps community for your constant guidance, support, cooperation and encouragement.
